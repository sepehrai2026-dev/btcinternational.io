Guide

Choosing a global exchange

Check availability first

Not every exchange operates in every country. Regulatory restrictions mean some platforms block users from certain jurisdictions entirely. Always verify your country is supported before creating an account.

Currency support

The best global exchanges support multiple fiat currencies and local payment methods. Look for your local currency pairs to avoid conversion fees. SEPA, Faster Payments, and local bank transfers are usually cheapest.

KYC requirements

Know Your Customer (KYC) requirements vary by exchange and jurisdiction. Most regulated exchanges require government ID verification. Some decentralized exchanges and certain P2P platforms require minimal or no KYC.
